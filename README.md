# Uber Trip Analysis

I developed an end-to-end Power BI dashboard to analyze an Uber trip data and extract actionable business insights related to demand patterns, revenue performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. I got the dataset through Olalekan Rasaq. 
I analyzed over 103,000 Uber trips, generating $1.6M in total booking value. The analysis highlights key patterns in demand, revenue, trip behavior, vehicle preference, payment methods, and location trends. 

### Notable insights include:
-🔹Higher revenue concentration during night trips
-🔹UberX as the most preferred vehicle type, and
-🔹Strong customer reliance on cash and Uber Pay. 

This project strengthened my ability to transform raw data into meaningful insights that support strategic and operational decision-making.
